FAQs

I am no longer enrolled in school, can I still book a session?

Yes!

What happens if I want to re-book my consultation?

Simply cancel the consultation on your user dashboard and then select the option on the left-hand side to re-book the appointment.

What goes on in a free consultation?

I get to know more about the student and their struggles so that I can create a customized studying plan to ensure they succeed.

What happens if I am not satisfied with the services?

You can request a refund on your user dashboard and the tutor will get back to you in 1-3 business days.

What is the maximum amount of sessions I can take?

I limit the number of sessions per week to a minimum of 3, but there really is no limit to the number of sessions you can take for the duration of the class.
